About

Buyers

Searching for a home can be a stressful experience especially if you aren't prepared and don't have the right people working for you. My goal isn't to just get you into a home as quick as possible but to have you as educated and informed as can be while getting into a home as efficiently as we can.

Before getting started on your search I will be able to connect with you with a mortgage professional that will assist you with getting pre-qualified. This will help determine what your budget is and I will be able to show you examples of homes that recently sold within that budget. This will give you a realistic expectation of what kind of home you may be eligible for in today's market.

After we know your budget we will determine what you absolutely need, what would be nice to have, and what you definitely do not want in a house. After we know what you're looking for I will be able to send you available listings and keep you updated with any new listings that come to market that match your wants and needs. This list of requirements may change over the course of your search and that's okay, that's all part of the search! At the end of the day we want you to be happy with where you choose to live.

We will then go on showings to make sure the house is what it seems in the listing photos and that it matches your requirements. I will also keep an eye out for any red flags that we should watch out for while we go through the home. If there is something we are not sure about I will be able to connect you with a professional that can investigate it further.

When you decide to write an offer I will provide guidance in making your offer as attractive as possible to the seller. I will also provide you with comparable sales to give an idea of what a winning bid would look like.

After an offer is accepted I will be there helping you ensure everything is ready for closing day! At the end of the day my goal is to keep you informed, on track, and excited about what could be one of the biggest investments of your life!

Sellers

If you are thinking about selling your home you likely have a number of questions and are wondering how you can get the most out of your home. I'm here to help guide you through the sale of your home while ensuring you do not leave money on the table!

We will help determine what changes you can make that will provide the most return on your investment. These can range from de-cluttering and de-personalizing, deep cleaning, a fresh coat of paint, or other select updates depending on your budget. Our end goal is to make sure you're happy after the sale of your home; there are some things in the market that we cannot control but we will focus on the things we can control to get you the most from your sale.

We can also connect you with professional staging companies that can make your home appeal to the largest audience of buyers possible while using professional photos and video to show your home in the best light possible for your listing.

When the listing is live we will continue to promote it through various avenues to get as much attention on your home as possible. You will be updated throughout the entire time the house is on the market as often as you require regarding the number of showings, general interest, etc. I will then present any offers to you and help you determine which one is best for you.

After you've accepted an offer I will be available to ensure everything goes smoothly leading up to closing day!
